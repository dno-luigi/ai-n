privacy

we take your privacy seriously. here's the simple truth about how we handle your information:

in plain terms:

  • we don't sell your data to anyone
  • we don't use cookies to track you
  • we don't store your personal information
  • we won't contact you unless you ask us to

why this matters:

in a world where your searches and data are often used against you, we believe in providing a safe alternative. our site is designed to respect your privacy while still delivering powerful AI-driven insights.

what we actually do:

  • we only collect what's necessary to make the site work
  • we use technical data to improve your experience
  • we implement strong security measures
  • we give you full control over your information

our promise to you:

the only time we'll ever use what you do on our site is to make your experience better. and if you ask us to send you something? well, that's the only time we'll reach out - and even then, only if you explicitly opt in.

tl;dr: we don't sell your data, we don't track you, and we don't store your information. your privacy is our priority.
